Online Bookings @ Impact Hub, Oakland

oakland.impacthub.net

Meet Impact Hub (IHO)

IHO is a rent-a-coworking space for entrepreneurs. They have monthly plans to share working spaces and they also provide venue rentals for seminars and conferences.

The Challenge

`Reduce calls to customer service with better User Experience`

Booking a venue and signing up, renewing or cancelling memberships results in a lot of phone calls to the IHO customer service. Our challenge was to reduce these phone calls by building a better UX on the IHO CRM portal.


Finding the Why.... User Research

We started with trying to understand Why ? , why is it so hard for IHO users to accomplish tasks on the website. We spoke to several users and went through customer support logs.


Aha Moment & our final solution

Simplicity, Intuitiveness was the key. Our research concluded that excessive noise on individual web pages and too many redirects to complete a single action was the key to confusing most users.Our goal was to make the user’s workflow

  • Clear
  • Simple
  • Efficient

Social Rider Sharing on Hertz Platform

hertz.com

Problem Statement

Hertz is a very popular car rental services. Hertz has been seeing a steady decline in their 1-2 day aka short term rentals due to the emergence of share based care rental services like zipcar etc. Your goal is to design a ride sharing product on top of the existing Hertz Infrastructure.

Finding what matters

We conducted user survey and interviews to understand our users and their attitude towards car rental services. To effectively analyze the data we collected, we created an affinity map to discover trends.
